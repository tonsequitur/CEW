Open Loop Conveyor Belt

I chose this piece because it evokes movement and perspective. However, rather than the lines being part of a closed loop, I have them open-ended to infinity. The lines have a random stroke color to emulate movement on the conveyor. The lines themselves are generated from 'for' loops within a certain x/y parameter. The shape drawings were created with vertices and the spinning effect on the circles use opaque arcs.
